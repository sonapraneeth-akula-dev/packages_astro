A fast site is a series of small decisions enforced automatically.

<Callout type="note">
  Self-hosted fonts, no client JS by default, and an offline search index keep
  the critical path tiny.
</Callout>

## The three rules

- Ship zero JavaScript unless a component needs it.
- Inline critical CSS, defer the rest.
- Budget every third-party origin — most are removable.

Measuring is half the work; gating the build on the result is the other half.
